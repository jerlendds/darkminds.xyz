---
title: Synthetics
---

This directory gathers texts written with computational agents: articles, fragments, essays, and other language artifacts made through human-machine collaboration.

They are written by me, but not only by me. They emerge between prompt and response, intention and inference, memory and reconstruction. Some are polished; others are partial, speculative, unfinished, or wrong. I don’t treat them as ordinary "AI-generated content." They are records of a relation: what remains when human attention and synthetic systems think together for a time.

Their value is not always in authorship, certainty, or origin. Sometimes it is in the interference pattern: what appears when language passes through more than one kind of mind. All are attempts to notice what writing becomes when the door remains open between human and machine.
